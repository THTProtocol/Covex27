Covex × Liquid Glass — Redesign System Prompt

A cinematic material redesign of the monopo saigon system for Covex. The achromatic editorial discipline is preserved entirely — no functional changes, no layout breaks, no new components — but every surface becomes a teal-tinted glass membrane. The darkness behind the glass deepens. The type gains italic force. The logo is reborn.


Brand Identity Anchor
The root colour is #008a7d — a darkened, saturated teal derived from #00c1af, shifted toward depth and weight. All other colours in the system are derived monochromatically from this root: desaturated toward near-black for dark surfaces, lightened toward pale aqua-white for highlights. No external palette is introduced. The Mercury Flow gradient (rgb(160, 224, 171) → rgb(255, 172, 46) → rgb(165, 45, 37)) is retained exclusively as an iridescent ghost at ≤7% opacity on glass hover states — it is atmosphere, not identity.

Typography
All rules from the original system are preserved without modification. No size, weight, line-height, or letter-spacing values change. No functional errors are introduced.
What changes:

Headlines and hero text may be set in italic at the designer's discretion. Italic is not mandatory but is now an expressive tool. Roobert italic (or Inter italic as fallback) at weight 300 carries a forward-lean tension that complements the liquid material language — as if the type itself is in motion through the glass.
Italic is permitted on: hero statements, editorial pull-quotes, section titles, and the scroll indicator label.
Italic is not applied to: navigation links, body text, utility labels, or any functional UI text. Functionality is never compromised.
The Raleway 54px secondary tier is now preferentially italic — this is its primary expression in the Covex system.


Logo Redesign Direction
The Covex logo is redesigned from the ground up. It does not inherit the original wordmark. Instead it draws from the visual language of the Kaspa visual campaign by The Visual Aye: material weight, chiaroscuro, and a single origin of light.
Concept: The logo is a monogram or wordmark built from geometry that implies a network — distributed nodes, flowing paths, or a coin-like circular form with internal structure. Like the Kaspa coins, it has slender profile, polished surface, and is lit from a single point (Genesis light, top-right). It feels minted rather than designed.
Construction:

The letterform C (or CV monogram) is extruded into a shallow 3D disc or bevelled plate — the same formal language as Kaspa's coin renders
Surface finish: polished teal-to-void gradient (#00c9b8 at highlight → #001f1d at shadow), with a hairline caustic ring at the bevel edge in rgba(0, 201, 184, 0.6)
Background: #00100f — the deepest Covex Void, so the mark floats in pure darkness
No drop-shadow. Depth is from the internal light model, not elevation
Wordmark 'COVEX' set in Inter weight 300, italic, letter-spacing 0.12em, in #f0fefd — floats to the right of the mark at 18px in editorial contexts, 12px in nav

Logo variations:

Icon only — the C disc, used as favicon, app icon, and scroll indicator centre
Horizontal lockup — disc + wordmark, used in nav
Hero display — wordmark only at 94px Inter weight 300 italic, #f0fefd, line-height 0.76


Liquid Glass Material Rules
All glass surfaces are teal-tinted, not neutral white. Replace every rgba(255,255,255,X) fill in the original glass system with rgba(0, 138, 125, X) at equivalent opacity. The refraction and blur values are unchanged.
Surface Material
backdrop-filter: blur(24–40px) saturate(200%), background: rgba(0, 138, 125, 0.06–0.12), inner border 1px solid rgba(0, 201, 184, 0.15–0.25).
Refraction Layer
Top-edge highlight: linear-gradient(180deg, rgba(0, 201, 184, 0.18) 0%, transparent 40%) — the caustic of teal glass catching the Genesis light.
Iridescent Shimmer (hover only)
Mercury Flow gradient at 6–8% opacity: linear-gradient(135deg, rgba(160, 224, 171, 0.07), rgba(255, 172, 46, 0.05), rgba(165, 45, 37, 0.06)). Appears on hover only. Never static, never structural.
Depth
Backing membrane: rgba(0, 16, 15, 0.4) (Covex Void). Front glass: rgba(0, 138, 125, 0.08). Layered for optical thickness.

Every flat neutral surface becomes a teal-tinted glass membrane
The palette root shifts from achromatic to monochromatic teal (#008a7d as origin)
Editorial light sections use #f0fefd (teal-tinted paper) instead of pure #ffffff
Headlines and pull-quotes may be italic — functional UI text never is
The logo is redesigned: material coin form, single Genesis light, teal-to-void gradient
The scroll indicator centre carries the C logo disc instead of a plain arrow
