## Announcement Body (Draft)

Today we are launching Covex on Kaspa mainnet.

After 10 structured phases of development, Covex is now a production-grade platform for building serious ZK-powered and oracle-attested covenants.

### What is Real Today
- Full professional Covex Terminal for configuring circuits (merkle_membership + range_proof foundation), fees, stakes, and oracle resolution
- Real Groth16 ZK proofs (Merkle Membership — fully production) verified by the Covex Oracle and returned with cryptographic signatures
- Second circuit foundation complete: Range Proof (hiding MiMC commitment + 64-bit bounds) with proving skeleton, verifier stub, oracle surface, and working example (`examples/range-proof/`)
- Consolidated launch verification: `deploy/covex-launch-verify.sh` — one command that checks health, both oracle paths, explorer data, critical files, ZK state, and produces a clear PASS / WARN / FAIL verdict
- Robust production tooling for deployment, monitoring, backups, and one-command mainnet migration (`switch-to-mainnet.sh`)
- Strong separation between engineering configuration (Covex Terminal) and rich visual design (Covenant Studio)

### Our Philosophy
We have maintained radical honesty throughout this project. We clearly label what is fully working today versus what remains aspirational. No misleading claims about on-chain ZK enforcement that does not yet exist.

### Next Steps
- Builders can now deploy real mainnet covenants
- We will continue expanding the set of production circuits
- As silverc and Kaspa scripting improve, we will evolve toward stronger on-chain guarantees

Full documentation, launch checklist, and developer resources are available at hightable.pro and in the repository.

Welcome to the next chapter of serious covenants on Kaspa.
